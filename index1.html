Our Alignment Method

Beyond the
Résumé

A qualified candidate and a perfect placement are two very different things. Our alignment process evaluates temperament, work rhythm, communication style, and the subtle cultural cues that determine whether someone will thrive in a given household — not just perform adequately in it.

We ask the questions other recruiters don't. Then we listen carefully to both sides of the equation.

We Evaluate
  • · Technical credentials & verified references
  • · Temperament & interpersonal communication style
  • · Adaptability to household culture & formality level
  • · Discretion record & confidentiality track record
  • · Long-term career arc & retention indicators
  • · Environment-specific expertise match
